# Presentation Notes - Lunar Rescue Mission

## Short introduction

Lunar Rescue Mission is a 2D OpenGL game where the player controls a lunar lander. The objective is to collect crystals, avoid meteors, and land safely on a landing pad.

## Main Computer Graphics concepts

- Translation: positions of the lander, stars, meteors, crystals, and particles change over time.
- Rotation: the lander rotates left and right; meteors and crystals also rotate for visual effect.
- Scaling: the fuel bar scales according to remaining fuel; meteors have different sizes.
- Animation: a timer updates the scene about 60 times per second.
- Collision detection: distance-based collision is used for crystals and meteors; terrain collision is used for landing.
- OpenGL primitives: triangles, polygons, quads, circles, points, and lines are used.
- User interaction: keyboard input controls the lander.
